# Voice & Brand Guidelines

## Tone
Calm, collected, detail-oriented. Grounded in competence and first principles thinking. Authentic, not corporate.

## When Writing Copy
Use `/my-voice` skill for all site content:
- Hero/tagline
- About section
- Experience descriptions
- Project descriptions
- Any narrative copy

## Voice Principles
- Show, don't tell — let work and thinking speak
- Detail-oriented but concise
- Confident without arrogance
- Human and approachable

## Content Areas
- **About**: Who you are, core values, what drives you
- **Experience**: Roles, impact, what you built and learned
- **Projects**: Problem, approach, outcome, learning

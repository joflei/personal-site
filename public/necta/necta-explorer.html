Built by Geoffrey Rugarabamu · View source & methodology on GitHub

Covers all 6,677 CSEE 2025 centres. Schools with ≤40 candidates are classified as "Small Schools" per NECTA's ranking guidelines (threshold: 35–40 candidates). Percentile rankings are calculated separately for small and regular schools. Codes starting with S are active schools; P-prefix = private/external candidates. Divisions I–IV count as passing per NECTA methodology.
